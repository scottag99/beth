Bookkeeping and Financial Services

I provide bookkeeping and financial services to local businesses and beyond. Running your business is hard, and bookkeeping can seem daunting. I love to help entreprenuers and small business owners get organized so they have a clear picture on the financial health of their business.

Qualified

I received both my Bachelors and Masters in Accounting from Texas A&M University while a member of their prestigious Fellows program. I have experience in public accounting during my time at PriceWaterhouseCoopers and private accounting while doing consulting and working in the oil and gas industry for Koch and Kinder Morgan.
